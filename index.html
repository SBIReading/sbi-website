About us

Smart Builders Innovation is a small, hands-on team delivering full outdoor and indoor upgrades, from groundwork to finish. We build and renovate:

  • Patios, porcelain paving, natural stone, raised beds, retaining walls
  • Sleepers, steps, paths, drive edging, garden borders and levels
  • New turfing, re-levelling gardens, weed control, hedge cutting and routine maintenance
  • Fencing and gates (timber & metal), front boundary solutions and privacy screens
  • Garden structures: sheds, pergolas, outdoor storage, custom sleeper planters
  • Garden lighting & irrigation (sprinklers / watering systems for lawns and beds)
  • Ponds, water features and bespoke waterfalls
  • New porches & porch repairs
  • Rendering, re-pointing brickwork, small structural alterations
  • Interior work: bathroom and kitchen refits, tiling, plastering, painting, flooring

We also work with qualified partners for certified electrics and plumbing, so we can bring power, lighting, outdoor sockets, pumps and drainage into the job without you having to coordinate multiple trades.

One team. Start to finish. No drama — just a clean, finished result.
